## Key Findings

### 1. Electronics Dominates (73.7%)
**$11,299.74 revenue** - Makes up nearly 3/4 of total revenue

### 2. Premium Customers = Higher Value
- Premium: $698.90 avg transaction
- Standard: $625.27 avg transaction
- **Premium customers 11.8% higher**

### 3. Revenue Imbalance
- Electronics: 73.7%
- Home & Garden: 20%
- Clothing: 4.7%
- Books: 1.6%

**Opportunity:** Grow underperforming categories (Clothing & Books)

### 4. Strong Repeat Customers
**73% repeat purchase rate** - Indicates good product-market fit

### 5. Data Quality: 100%
✓ Zero missing values  
✓ Zero duplicates  
✓ All outliers verified  

---

## Recommendations

1. **Convert Standard → Premium** 
   - Target high-value Standard customers
   - Expected impact: +$750-1,500 revenue

2. **Grow Underperforming Categories**
   - Test promotions on Clothing & Books
   - Goal: Increase from 6.3% to 15% of revenue

3. **Loyalty Program**
   - Increase repeat rate from 73% to 85%+
   - Expected impact: +$2,300 annual

4. **Premium Product Bundles**
   - Higher margins on Electronics + premium products
   - Expected: +3-5% margin improvement
